# Document and enforce phase-one CI safety policy

Status: ready-for-agent
Category: enhancement

## What to build

Make the phase-one Forgejo runner safety boundary visible and testable: CI is for non-mutating repository validation only. The Runner VM must not become an automation principal for live infrastructure, must not be an age Recipient, must not decrypt SOPS, and must not receive deployment-runner labels or direct management reachability assumptions.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Documentation states that phase-one Forgejo Actions are limited to non-mutating repository validation.
- [ ] Documentation states that the Runner VM is not an age Recipient and does not decrypt Fortress SOPS secrets.
- [ ] Documentation states that live Host, VM, NAS, PBS, and Service convergence remains Operator-owned.
- [ ] The declared runner labels describe validation capabilities and do not imply deployment or management authority.
- [ ] Tests or policy checks guard against accidentally adding runner SOPS recipient material as part of phase-one runner work.
- [ ] Tests or policy checks guard against declaring job container-runtime socket exposure without an explicit future design change.
- [ ] Operator-facing docs explain that deployment runners require a separate later design.
